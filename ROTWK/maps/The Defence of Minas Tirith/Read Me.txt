In This map you have to defend the city of Minas Tirith against Mordor!

This Map Includes:
- Assault waves
- Grond
- Nazgûls
- Rohirrims
- The undead army
- Custom Objectives

HOW TO PLAY:
-!!!Play on the Top spot!!!
-Choose the color (it can't be changed by any scripts)
-You should restart the game before and after playing to avoid coding errors
-Choose a custom hero
-Have fun !

Compatibility:
This map is compatible with both BFME 2 and ROTWK but it has only been tested in bfme 2 so the map might
not be balanced in ROTWK (I don't think so but i prefer telling you).
