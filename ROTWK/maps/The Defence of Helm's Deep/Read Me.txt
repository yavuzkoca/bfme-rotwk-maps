In This map you have to defend Helm's Deep against Isengard!

This Map Includes:
- Assault waves
- Rams
- Mines
- Rohirrim reinforcement
- Ents
- Custom Objectives
- The Faction of Rohan brought back

HOW TO PLAY:
-!!!Play on the Top spot!!!
-Choose the color (it can't be changed by any scripts)
-You should restart the game before and after playing to avoid coding errors
-Choose a custom hero
-Have fun !

Compatibility:
This map is compatible with  ROTWK Patch 2.02 only.
